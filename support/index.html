Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a gig?
Tap the Log tab (the + icon) in the tab bar, search for the artist, fill in the details — venue, date, ratings, and an optional review — then tap Save. Your gig will appear in your diary and, if set to public, in your followers' feeds.
Can I log gigs I went to in the past?
Yes — you can log any gig regardless of when it happened. Just select the correct date when logging. There's no limit on how far back you can go, so feel free to build up your full gig history.
How do I find and follow friends?
Use the Search tab to search for users by username. Tap their profile and tap Follow. You'll then see their public gig logs in your feed.
How do I change my genre preferences?
Go to Profile → Settings → Genre Preferences to update the genres used to personalise your Discover feed. Your recommendations will update immediately.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data — your profile, gig logs, photos, comments, follows, and everything else. This action cannot be undone.
How do I report a user?
Tap the three-dot menu on any post or profile and select Report. We review all reports and take action where our community guidelines are breached.
My gig isn't showing up in the Discover tab — why?
The Discover tab shows upcoming events sourced from Ticketmaster and other providers. Independent shows, DICE-only events, and Resident Advisor listings may not appear. You can still log these gigs manually — just search for the artist and enter the details yourself.
How do I share my gig to Instagram?
Open any logged gig from your diary, tap the share icon, and select "Share to Instagram Stories". Banded creates a ticket-stub style share card with your gig details that you can post directly to your story.
How do I cancel Banded Pro?
Go to iPhone Settings → your name (Apple ID) → Subscriptions → Banded Pro → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Email hello@banded.uk with a description of what happened, what you expected to happen, and your iPhone model and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are really helpful if you can include them.
